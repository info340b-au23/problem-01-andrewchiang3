Mort

Mort is a mouse lemur in the lemur kingdom of madgascar, he is shown in the movie series Madagascar and the spinoff tv show All Hail King Julian. He often spends his time worshipping and serving the king lemurs, King Julian. He has a particular liking to King Julian's feet.
